Your Kitten's Health Is Our Promise

At Winston James Ragdoll For Adoption, every kitten is raised with unwavering dedication to health and temperament. Our comprehensive health guarantee covers full veterinary examinations, age-appropriate vaccinations, deworming, and a written health record — so you can welcome your new family member with complete confidence and peace of mind.

We believe that lifelong breeder support is the cornerstone of ethical Ragdoll kitten adoption. From the moment you reserve your kitten through every milestone of their life, our family is here to guide, reassure, and celebrate alongside you — because a healthy kitten is just the beginning of a beautiful journey.

Health & Wellness Promise

Our Comprehensive Health Guarantee

Every Winston James Ragdoll kitten is raised with meticulous care, backed by a transparent health assurance framework designed to give you complete peace of mind.

Written Health Coverage

We provide a detailed written guarantee covering congenital and hereditary health conditions for a defined period after adoption. This document outlines our commitment to the lifelong well-being of your Ragdoll companion and serves as your assurance of our breeding standards.

Vaccination & Wellness Protocol

All kittens receive age-appropriate core vaccinations, routine deworming, and a complete wellness examination before joining their new families. You will receive a full veterinary record detailing every step of their preventative care journey.

Veterinary Examination Standards

Each kitten undergoes a thorough veterinary health check within days of going home. Our independent veterinarians assess cardiac, respiratory, and structural health, ensuring your Ragdoll kitten meets the highest benchmarks for breed-specific wellness.

Lifetime Breeder Support

Our relationship does not end at adoption. We offer ongoing guidance on nutrition, behavior, and health for the lifetime of your Ragdoll. Whether you have a question in the first week or years later, we are just a call or message away.
